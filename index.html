🌟 About Us

Dedicated to creating a more inclusive society for people who are abled differently

📖 Our Story

The Roy Ndung'u Foundation is inspired by the life of Roy Ndung'u, born with Erb's Palsy and later diagnosed with Epilepsy. His journey of resilience and determination has shaped our mission to create a world where every person abled differently can thrive with dignity, respect, and equal opportunities.

🎯 Our Mission

We are committed to creating awareness, eliminating stigma, and providing comprehensive support services for individuals abled differently and their families. Through advocacy, education, and direct services, we work to build an inclusive society where everyone belongs.

🌍 Our Vision

A society where people abled differently are valued, included, and empowered to reach their full potential. We envision communities free from discrimination, where accessibility and inclusion are the norm, not the exception.

💪 Our Programs

Comprehensive initiatives designed to support and empower individuals abled differently

🌿 Afya Bora, Maisha Bora, Jamii Bora Initiative

Comprehensive health and wellness programs promoting better health outcomes for individuals abled differently and their families within the community.

🤝 Care for Caregivers Workshop

Support and training programs for caregivers, providing them with essential skills, resources, and emotional support to better care for their loved ones.

💻 ICT & Digital Skills Centre

Technology training programs designed to bridge the digital divide and provide people abled differently access to modern digital tools and skills.

💰 Financial & Business Literacy Training

Building financial independence through comprehensive business and financial education for people abled differently and their families.

📢 Advocacy & Awareness

Raising awareness and advocating for the rights of individuals abled differently to foster an inclusive and supportive society.
